Focus Areas:

  • Try to keep the distance between you and pistols whenever possible I noticed a few times you would accidentally close the distance between you and the upgraded pistols and this would make it easier for them in the fight.
  • Accidentally peeking before your utility lands exposes you to the enemy and thus putting you at a disadvantageous fight.

  • Sometimes exposing yourself to multiple positions at once.
  • Tap slightly slower with the usp to make sure all your bullets are completely accurate.
  • Slightly dangerous pathing, always try to choose a path that exposes you as little to the enemies while giving you as much cover as possible.
  • Only pull out your knife when you know you are 100% safe and no one can shoot at you while rotating, this got you punished.
